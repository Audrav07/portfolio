About Me

Hey, my name is Audrey. I graduated from University of Hawai'i at Manoa with a BA in Communications. By day I work in an office at a medical device company. After hours you'll find me in the mountains or behind a computer learning how to code from University of Denver College coding bootcamp. I will graduate from the program in April 2018 as a full stack web developer.
I am a lover of all things fluffy and the great outdoors. I have worked in a variety of sectors including: event planning, non-profit, medical, editorial, and fashion. What I lack in coding experience I make up for in spunk and tenacity. I am a firecracker who is willing to go the extra lengths to make sure the client is happy.
